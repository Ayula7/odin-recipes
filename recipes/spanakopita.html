Spanakopita

This is our family recipe for Spanakopita, a great Greek dinner recipe, which can also be enjoyed cold!

  • 1 bunch of spring onions
  • 1kg of spinach
  • 1,5 packages of Feta cheese ~220g
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 roll of puff pastry
  • 1 Tbsp. of milk
  • Sesame seeds
  • Nutmeg
  1. Cut the spring onions into rings and sauté until translucent.
  2. Sauté spinach, cut coarsely, squeeze out water, add onion.
  3. Cut the puff pastry in half, put it in a baking dish, fill it with the filling and put the other half of the puff pastry on top.
  4. Spread puff pastry with milk, then sprinkle with sesame seeds.
  5. Bake at 200 degree Celsius, convection oven for 20min.
